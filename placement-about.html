About Placements

Bridging the gap between academia and industry excellence through student-centric initiatives and robust corporate linkages.

PC

Placements Cell

The institution firmly believes that placements are the major benchmark. Started in 1995, we have achieved 18500+ cumulative offers.

V

Vision

To emerge as a most preferred destination for all corporates to hire globally competent and ethically strong engineering talent.

M

Mission

Nurture talent, identify career options, enhance employability, and facilitate futuristic skills acquisition.

IT

Infrastructure & Training

State-of-the-art physical and computing infrastructure. Soft-skills training, technical talks, and mock assessments.

Placement Process

1

Network & Invitation

Establishing networks and building relations with potential recruiters. Invitations are sent to visit the Institute.

2

Coordination

Dates for campus visits are communicated considering factors like student preference and job profiles.

3

Pre-Placement Talks

Companies are encouraged to give presentations before the commencement of the recruitment process.

4

Selection Process

Recruiters conduct aptitude tests, group discussions, technical and HR interviews to identify top talent.

5

Administrative Activities

Conducting general follow-up, joining formalities and administrative coordination for selected students.
