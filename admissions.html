📝 Academic Eligibility

1. Through KEA / COMED-K

FOR GENERAL MERIT CANDIDATES: Passed in 2nd PUC / 12 Std / Equivalent Examination with English as one of the Language and obtained a Minimum of 45% of Marks in aggregate in Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry / Bio Technology / Biology / Computer Science / Electronics as optional subjects.


FOR SC/ST & OBC (Cat-I, 2A, 2B, 3A 3B) CATEGORY: Passed in 2nd PUC / 12 Std / Equivalent Examination with English as one of the Language and obtained a Minimum of 40% of Marks in aggregate in Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.


Note: Marks in Biotechnology/Biology/Computer Science / Electronics will be considered in place of Chemistry if Chemistry marks are less.

Entrance Examination: KEA / COMED-K. For Management Admissions: Students must take any one Entrance Exam.

2. Through Lateral Entry Scheme

Passed Qualifying Examination (Diploma or equivalent) with minimum 45% (General) or 40% (SC/ST/BC of Karnataka) in all subjects of final year (5th & 6th Sem).

Aggregate % = (Sum of marks in 5th & 6th Sem X 100) / (Sum of maximum marks in 5th & 6th Sem)
