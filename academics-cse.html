Computer Science & Engineering

Empowering the next generation of computing leaders through innovation and best teaching practices.

About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BMSCE is committed to providing quality education in the ever-evolving field of computing. We focus on empowering students with a strong foundation in core computer science principles while encouraging innovation and research in frontier areas.

Vision

To be a model centre for education and training in the frontier areas of Computer Science and Engineering.

Mission

  • To Educate and Empower the students in Computer Science via best practices.
  • To enhance skills for higher studies and research.
  • To foster innovation and nurture entrepreneurship.
