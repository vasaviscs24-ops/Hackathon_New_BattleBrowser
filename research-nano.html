B S Narayan Centre for Nano-Materials & Displays

Launched on 30th September 2019, the CND stands as a premier interdisciplinary hub for pioneering Display Technology and Nanomaterials innovation in India. Dedicated to catering the display community through novel innovation, research, and world-class commercialisation activities.
Advanced Fabrication Unit
Characterization Lab

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: Dedicated to cater display community and nano-materials area through novel innovation and commercialisation.
  • Mission: To become the nodal point for entire display activity in India and a world-class R&D centre.

Research Priorities

  • Liquid crystals and LCD phase transitions
  • Photoisomerization and optical storage devices
  • Electrochemical sensors & Biowaste based nanomaterials
  • Cancer cell imaging & Antibacterial studies
  • CO2 adsorption & Waste-to-wealth technology

Major Facilities

  • Class-1000 Cleanroom: Specialized for materials fabrication.
  • LCD Laboratory: Full cycle from glass cleaning to LCD panels.
  • Photo Alignment Unit: For aligning nano-layers with precision.
  • Electrochemical Lab: Full suite for coin cell and battery fabrication.

Services Offered

  • Clean room facilities for display industry needs.
  • Characterization services for industry and academic institutes.
  • Photoalignment based nano-layers production.
  • LC cells supply and consultancy for LCD industries.
  • Porous nanomaterials supply and collaborative R&D.
