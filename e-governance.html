E-Governance

"Empowering the Future of Institutional Excellence through Seamless, Cutting-Edge Digital Governance."

Transforming Administration

The E-governance committee was formally established in the year 2019 with the singular, profound vision of revolutionizing the institute's governance systems using state-of-the-art technologies.

This ambitious transformation is driven by developing bespoke, tailor-made digital solutions addressing the existing operational frameworks within the college. The overarching scope initially encompasses critical fields such as examinations, admissions, daily departmental operations, Management Information Systems (MIS), and ensuring stakeholder inclusion in a seamlessly structured, staged manner.

Furthermore, the deeply committed team constantly aims at strategizing and facilitating robust infrastructure for deploying cutting-edge applications, ensuring undeniably frictionless, radically efficient administration.
